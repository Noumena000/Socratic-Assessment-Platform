See How It Works

Here’s an example of how the system analyzes a paper and generates a Socratic dialogue to test understanding.

Sample Paper

Title: The Ethics of AI in Healthcare

In healthcare, autonomy is a critical principle. Patients have the right to make their own decisions about treatment, even if those decisions go against medical advice. However, autonomy must be balanced with beneficence, as healthcare providers have a duty to promote patient well-being. For example, a patient may refuse a life-saving treatment due to personal beliefs, but the provider must ensure the patient fully understands the consequences of their choice.

Detected Concepts

Concepts found in the paper that would be tested:

Autonomy Beneficence

Socratic Dialogue

Example questions the system would ask based on the paper’s content:

System

Define 'autonomy' as used in your paper.

Student

Autonomy is the right of patients to make their own decisions about their treatment, even if those decisions go against medical advice.

System

How does your paper apply the concept of autonomy?

Student

The paper applies autonomy by emphasizing that patients have the right to refuse treatment, even if it may lead to worse health outcomes.

System

Can autonomy ever be limited? Explain using an example from your paper.

Student

Yes, autonomy can be limited if the patient's decision would harm others. For example, a patient with a contagious disease may not have the autonomy to refuse quarantine.

Security & Integrity

Built to Prevent AI Cheating

The platform includes multiple layers of protection to ensure students demonstrate genuine understanding and academic integrity.

🔒

Copy/Paste Disabled

Students cannot copy or paste text into the assessment interface, forcing them to engage with the material directly and type their own responses.

🗣️

Socratic Dialogue

Students must explain their paper's content in their own words through a guided dialogue, revealing true understanding rather than regurgitating AI-generated text.

🚩

Red Flag Detection

Inconsistencies between a student's paper and their dialogue responses are automatically flagged for faculty review, such as when a student uses a term in their paper but cannot define it.

👀

Faculty Oversight

Faculty review all dialogue and evidence before making final assessments. The platform supports instructor judgment rather than replacing it with automated grading.
